Business Analysis and Process Improvement

Investigated a 13.6% decline in corporate segment profitability through hypothesis-driven business analysis, uncovering product, pricing, and geographic factors behind profit leakage and delivering five prioritized initiatives to achieve 25% profit growth.

Situation

Corporate segment profitability fell by 13.6% in 2022 despite increases in sales, orders, and customers.

Task

Identify the drivers of declining profitability and formulate strategies to achieve 25% profit growth by the end of 2023.

Action

Performed end-to-end business analysis using stakeholder mapping, issue trees, hypothesis testing, profitability analysis, and geographic performance analysis to validate root causes and prioritize improvement initiatives.

Result

Identified that Technology and Furniture profitability declined by 48.7% and 65.7%, uncovered persistent losses across nine states, and developed five prioritized recommendations to improve profitability performance.
